About Climate Change

Climate change refers to long-term shifts and alterations in temperature and weather patterns. While some changes are natural, human activities have been the primary driver of climate change since the 19th century, mainly due to burning fossil fuels like coal, oil, and gas.

Key Effects of Climate Change

  • Rising global temperatures
  • Melting polar ice caps
  • More frequent extreme weather events
  • Rising sea levels
  • Threats to biodiversity

It is crucial to take action by reducing carbon emissions, promoting renewable energy, and adopting sustainable practices to combat climate change.
